FAQ - Frequently Asked Questions

What is PoolBNB?

PoolBNB is an online platorm that connects people through events.
Whether you're looking to host an event or attend one, PoolBNB makes it easy to find and connect with others who share your interests.

Is there an age requirement to use PoolBNB?

Yes, you must be at least 18 years old to use PoolBNB.

How do I create an account on PoolBNB?

To create an account on PoolBNB, simply click on the "Register" button on the homepage and fill out the registration form with your details.
Once you've submitted the form, you will be able to log in and start using the platform.

How do I create an event on PoolBNB?

To create an event on PoolBNB, log in to your account and click on the "Create Event" button.
Fill out the event details, including the date, time, location, and description, and submit the form.
Once you've submitted the form, the event will be listed on the platform for others to view and book.

How do I view my bookings on PoolBNB?

To view your bookings on PoolBNB, log in to your account and click on the "My Bookings" page.
This will show you a list of your upcoming and past bookings, along with the details of each event.
