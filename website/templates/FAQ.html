FAQ = Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about hosting and booking on PoolBNB!

What is PoolBNB?

PoolBNB is a platform that connects hosts and guests through private pool-side events and experiences. Create an event, invite people, or discover unique gatherings near you.

Who can use PoolBNB?

You must be 18 years or older to host or book. Some events may require verification if requested by the host.

How do I create an account?

Click Register and complete the form. You can then log in, create events, and book experiences immediately.

How do I create an event?

Go to Create Event, add title, date, capacity, price, amenities (e.g., heated or indoor), and house rules. Publish to make it discoverable.

Can I reschedule or cancel my booking?

Yes, availability depends on the host's policy. You can request a date change in My Bookings. Cancellations follow the event's listed cancellation window.

How do payments work?

Guests pay securely at checkout. Funds are held until the event start, then released to the host (minus platform fees) within 3-5 business days.

What is the refund policy?

Refunds follow the host's cancellation policy. If an event is cancelled by the host or due to verified severe weather, guests receive a full refund.

Safety and House Rules

Each event lists its own rules (capacity limits, alcohol policy, noise curfew, no-glass near pool, etc.). By booking, you agree to follow them. Hosts may remove guests who break rules.

Weather

Hosts can specify a wet-weather plan (reschedule or covered area). If severe weather makes the event unsafe and no alternative is possible, bookings are refunded.

Damage and Liability

Guests are responsible for any damage they cause. Hosts should disclose any security deposit or bond in the listing. For incidents, contact support immediately.
